Apricot Balls¶

Ingredients¶

  • 3/4 C raw cashews
  • 3/4 C raw almonds
  • 1/4 C pitted Medjol dates
  • 3/2 C dried apricots
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1/4 C shredded coconut
  • 1 T grated orange zest
  • 3/2 T grated fresh ginger

Directions¶

  1. In a food processor, blend all the ingredients except the ginger until homogeneous.
  2. Blend in the ginger.
  3. Shape into balls.

Notes¶

Makes about 20 tablespoon-sized balls.
