Red Cabbage Salad¶

Ingredients¶

  • 1 medium head red cabbage (about 1.5 kg), grated
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 t caraway seeds
  • 1 apple, grated
  • 2/3 C balsamic vinegar
  • 2 T olive oil

Directions¶

  1. Combine the cabbage, salt, and caraway seeds in a large mixing bowl, and thoroughly squeeze the mixture for several minutes to soften the cabbage and release its juice.
  2. Mix in the rest of the Ingredients.
  3. If you have the time, chill the salad for several hours before serving.

Notes¶

Serves 6.
