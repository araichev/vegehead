Anise Drink
============

Ingredients
-----------
- 1   L   water
- 1   T   anise seeds
- 1   T   flax seeds
- 1   T   honey
- 1       lemon, juiced


Directions
-----------
#. Boil the water and anise seeds for 20 minutes.  
#. Add the flax seeds and boil for another 10 minutes.  
#. Remove from heat, strain out the seeds, and stir in honey and lemon.
