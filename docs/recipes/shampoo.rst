Shampoo
=========
| **Makes**: ½ c
| **Equipment**: funnel, an old shampoo bottle


Ingredients
------------
- ½ c castille soap
- 10–15 drops of your favorite essential oil of choice, e.g. lavender or rose

Instructions
------------
#. Pour everything into the shampoo bottle with a funnel, shake the bottle, and use.
