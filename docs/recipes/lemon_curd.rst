Lemon Curd
==============
**Serves**: 2 /
**Equipment**: sauce pan


Ingredients
-----------
- 4     T   butter
- 1/2   c   lemon juice
- 5/2   T   maple syrup
- 4         eggs


Directions
----------
#. Melt the butter in a sauce pan over low heat
#. Mix in the rest of ingredients, eggs last, and stir periodically until thick
#. Chill then serve
